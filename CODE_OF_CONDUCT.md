# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence without attacking people.
- Give actionable feedback and accept correction professionally.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.
- Stop behavior when a maintainer identifies it as disruptive or unsafe.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption,
and retaliation are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile.
Include relevant links or screenshots. Maintainers will limit disclosure to
people needed to assess the report and may warn, temporarily restrict, or ban a
participant. Decisions may be appealed once by providing new relevant context.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project in
public spaces.
